La Nostra Storia

Three Generations of Flavor

Since 1972, the Russo family has served Milano with recipes carried across generations — slow-cooked sauces, hand-pulled pasta, and wines selected from small Italian estates. Every plate is a tribute to the Italian table, where food is family.
